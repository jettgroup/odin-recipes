Guacamole

Fresh Table-side Guacamole

Ingredients
  • Avacados
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Jalepeno
  • Lime
  • Salt & Pepper
Steps
  • Slice avacados in half and remove pit. Scoop middle part out away from skin and dispose of skin and pit.
  • Chop onion, jalepeno, and tomatoes. Add to bowl with avacado
  • Slice lime in half and squeeze juice into bowl.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Stir, mashin up avacado until desired consistency.
  • Serve with tortilla chips.

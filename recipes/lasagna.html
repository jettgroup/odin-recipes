Baked Lasagna

Ingredients
  • Lasagna Noodles
    • Ricotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef
    • Parmasan Cheese Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning
Steps
  • Boil Noodles and drain. Set aside
  • Season and brown ground beef.
  • Layer sauce, then noodles, then ground beef, then ricotta cheese topped with mozzerella cheese topped with parmasan cheese 3 times into casserole dish.
  • Top with remaining shredded mozzerella and grated parmasan cheese.
  • Bake in oven preheated to 350 for 45 min.
